Introducing Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is from the anime series "Jujutsu Kaisen".

He has baby blue eyes that can surely attract anime fans.

About 6'3 ft tall, the standard of everyone.

A teacher ('sensei' in Japanese) at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College.

Gojo is often referred to as the “Strongest” or the “Handsome” among his colleagues and students.

His signature move is called the “Infinite Void” which creates a sphere that erases anything within it.
